## About Me

I'm Nathan Johnson, also known as NattyJ_. I'm an Operations Director working across community operations, digital media, communications, online platform management, and technology-led communities.

My work focuses on the practical side of building healthy online communities and digital experiences: organised teams, clear communication, audience trust, public relations, project coordination, and sustainable community growth.

I'm currently Operations Director at [Acheron Interactive](https://acheroninteractive.com/), supporting operations across MineSuperior, Daedric Networks, and EarthboundMC.
